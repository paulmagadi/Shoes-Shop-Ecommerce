About Us

Samarkand is a modern Uzbek restaurant located in San Francisco. We aim to bring the rich flavors and textures of Uzbek food to the Bay Area. Our goal is to provide our guests with a warm, inviting atmosphere that reflects the fusion of tradition and contemporary design.
